Our Story

Mzansi Memorabilia is a sport memorabilia retail store located in Umhlanga, KwaZulu-Natal. It was founded in 2025 by Sizwe Ngcobo and Luvuyo Sigcau. Their aim was to create a platform where fans can collect authentic local sports memorabilia, physical items that represent South Africa’s rich sporting history. They have various collectables such as game-worn jerseys, match balls, game-worn boots and more from different sports such as football (soccer), rugby, athletics and more.

Mission

Mzansi Memorabilia aims to provide fans with authentic South African sports memorabilia, while telling the stories behind South Africa’s rich sporting history.

Vision

Mzansi Memorabilia aims to become South Africa’s number one store for sports memorabilia and build a worldwide audience.
