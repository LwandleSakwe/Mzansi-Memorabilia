Our Story

Mzansi Memorabilia was founded in 2025 in Umhlanga, KwaZulu-Natal by Sizwe Ngcobo and Luvuyo Sigcau.

The idea was to give South African sports fans access to authentic, high-quality memorabilia that represents the country’s rich sporting history.

From signed jerseys to match-used equipment, every item tells a story and connects fans to unforgettable moments in sport.

Our Mission

Mzansi Memorabilia aims to provide fans with authentic South African sports memorabilia, while telling the stories behind South Africa’s rich sporting history.

Our Vision

Mzansi Memorabilia aims to become South Africa’s number one store for sports memorabilia and build a worldwide audience.
